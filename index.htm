Biography

Feng Shan received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Southeast University, Nanjing, China in 2015. He is currently an Assistant Professor at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University. He was a Visiting Scholar at the School of Computing and Engineering, University of Missouri-Kansas City, Kansas City, MO, USA, from 2010 to 2012. His research interests are in the areas of energy harvesting, wireless power transfer, algorithm design and analysis.
